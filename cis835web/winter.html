This Is Winter. We've had her for 12 years now or so. She is our oldest cat and used to be the nicest.

As Winter got older, she started to be meaner to the other cats as well as us. And it's such a shame because compared to Lucia, Winter was pleasant.

Old age is making Winter old and orneary, but I still love her in spite of her oncoming Alzheimer's. She holds a spot in my heart since she's been with us since the old house. May she live more years.
